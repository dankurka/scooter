\subsection{Detailed Description}
Routines for controlling the onboard Analog-Digital converter. 



\begin{Code}\begin{verbatim} #include <adc.h> 
\end{verbatim}\end{Code}



This Routines are for initializing the ADC and selecting the analog input channel of the multiplexer. After a channel has be activated, a single conversion may be performed by calling \doxyref{read\_\-adc()}{p.}{group__ADC_g44aa940d70eb32e3293a0824943927f8}.
